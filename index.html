FlowSurge AI is a cloud-based SaaS platform powered by agentic AI, designed specifically for specialty dental practices to streamline operations, reduce administrative burden, and maximize patient engagement.

What We Do

We provide an intelligent system that automates patient flow management and front office operations using advanced agentic AI technology, helping practices operate at peak efficiency.

Problems We Solve

Reduce no-shows, optimize appointment scheduling, eliminate manual administrative tasks, and ensure every treatment chair is utilized throughout the day.

Target Audience

Independent specialty dental practices including Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons, Orthodontists, Periodontists, Endodontists, and Prosthodontists.
